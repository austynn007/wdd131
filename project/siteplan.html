GreenRoots Community Garden - Site Plan

Site Name

GreenRoots Community Garden

This name evokes growth ("GreenRoots") and emphasizes the collective nature ("Community Garden") of the initiative. It aims to convey a sense of natural vitality and communal effort, which are central to the project's mission.

Optional Domain Availability: greenrootsgarden.org

Site Purpose

The GreenRoots Community Garden website will serve as a central hub to foster a vibrant community through shared gardening. It will provide essential resources, details on upcoming events, and a dedicated platform for members to connect, share knowledge, and learn from one another. The site aims to support both novice and experienced gardeners within our local community.

Scenarios

Here are some questions a typical site visitor might ask, driving the content and functionality of the website:

  • Scenario 1: New Gardener Seeking Guidance
    "I'm new to gardening; where can I find reliable information and resources on what to plant in my local climate and soil conditions, and how to get started?"
  • Scenario 2: Prospective Member Seeking Enrollment Information
    "I'm interested in joining the GreenRoots Community Garden. How do I sign up for a plot, what are the membership requirements, and what is the current availability?"
  • Scenario 3: Current Member Looking for Activities
    "I'm a current garden member. What upcoming workshops, social events, or volunteer opportunities are scheduled for the garden members in the next month?"

Color Schema

The chosen colors reflect the natural and community-focused theme of the garden.

  • Primary Accent Color: #228B22 (Forest Green)

    This deep green will be used for main headings (h1, h2), important calls-to-action, navigation links on hover, and other key accent elements to convey nature, growth, and a sense of calm.

  • Secondary Background/Text Color: #F5F5DC (Beige/Cream)

    This warm, neutral tone will be used for the main body background, paragraph text, and general content areas. It provides excellent readability and a natural, earthy feel, complementing the green accents.

Typography

Font choices are aimed at readability and conveying the site's friendly, grounded nature.

  • Headings (h1, h2, h3, etc.): 'Roboto Slab', serif

    Example: This is a Heading in Roboto Slab.

    A sturdy and approachable slab serif font, ideal for titles and section headings. It offers good readability and a modern yet classic feel that aligns with the garden's theme.

  • Body Text (paragraphs, lists, general content): 'Open Sans', sans-serif

    Example: This is body text in Open Sans. It is clear and easy to read.

    A highly versatile and legible sans-serif font, perfect for longer blocks of text. Its clean design ensures a comfortable reading experience for all site visitors.
